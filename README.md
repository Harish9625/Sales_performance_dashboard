#📊 Sales & Revenue Performance Dashboard (Looker Studio)
This repository hosts the documentation and live links for my interactive Business Intelligence dashboard created using Looker Studio. The project analyzes a retail dataset containing over $2.2M in sales.
🚀 Live Dashboard Link
👉 **[Click Here to Interact with the Live Dashboard] https://datastudio.google.com/reporting/4b8eebeb-e269-472c-9d23-0c73004221bd
🛠️ Features & Controls
Dynamic Regional Filters: Dropdown menus allowing business leaders to filter the entire dashboard by specific sales regions with a single click.
Core Business KPIs: Real-time tracking of Total Sales ($2.2M+), Total Profit, and Total Quantity Sold.
Logistics Analysis: Visualization breakdown tracking performance across different shipping modes.
💡 Business Impact
Strategic Planning: Enables regional managers to spot dipping revenue zones instantly and execute targeted market promotions.
Supply Chain Optimization: Highlighted 'Standard Class' as the primary shipping driver, helping logistics teams allocate resources efficiently.
